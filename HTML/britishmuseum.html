British Museum

The British Museum is one of the biggest and well-known museums in the world. The museum opened for the first time on 15 of January 1759 and has been driven since. The museum is well-known for the culture exhibition such as old culture treasures from Asia, Africa and Europe. In this museum you will see and learn how things were back in the days and it's an unforgettable experience.

The museum has a lot of departments that will wake up your interests and intelligence. It has 13 departments like “Department of Ancient Egypt and Sudan”, “Department of Ancient Near East”, “Department of Asia”, “Department of coins and medals”, “Department of Ethnography”, “Department of Greek and Roman Antiquities”, “Department of Prehistory and Europe”, “Department of Prints and Drawings”, “Department of Convervation”, “Documentation and Science”, “Department of Education” and “Department of Libraries and Archives.” It's a really interesting department and perfect for everyone!

We do a lot of tours like “Desire, love, identity - an LGBTQ0+ tour of the british museum”, “Around the world in 90 minutes tour”, And “Out of hours tour: an introduction to the british museum.” Perfect for school-trips, families, and if you want to be at the museum with someone.

The museum also arrange a lot of events. A few of them cost money, and some of them are actually FREE. That´s a great opportunity to learn thing without spending money. Come and visit our shops and cafes in the British Museum. WELCOME!
