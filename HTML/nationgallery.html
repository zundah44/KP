Nation Gallery

The National Gallery is in Trafalgar Square in London. Here in the Nation Gallery you can see the most famous paintings in the world! Here you will find famous paintings from the 13th to the 19th century, including several masterpieces such as Van Gogh, Rembrandt, Botticelli and Monet. The Nation Gallery is the UK's most significant museum of older British and European art.

The museum was founded in 1824 and has since been a very nice tourist museum. Here we hold both events and tours. The museum is a great destination for art lovers and those who want to explore history through the works of art. You can admire everything from beautiful Renaissance paintings to impressive masterpieces.
