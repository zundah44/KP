Some facts about Tower Brigde

  • The Brigde is 224 m long and 65 m high
  • Was built by the engineer Sir Harace Jones
  • Started builing in 1886, finished in 1894
  • The brigde is 130 years old

Tower bridge is an iconic suspension bridge known for its unique design with two towers connected by a bridge section that can be raised to allow boats to enter. Tower bridge is one of the most famous buildings in London and the Olympics have been held there.
